About

I'm a creative web developer with a strong focus on UI/UX. As such, I like to build user interfaces that take accessibility and user preferences into account for the best possible web apps, trying to improve every user's life. I'm fascinated by modern technologies, which usually makes me one of the people driving innovation and introducing new technics to the people I'm working with.

I like to collaborate with UI/UX designers, developers and users to create high-quality full-fledged solutions from design to production. As a passionate kind of person, going halfhearted is not in my nature. I'm always committed one hundred percent to give my best and learn from my mistakes.

I'm also a father of two boys. My free time is usually split between family activities and various sports. I'm into martial arts, have been doing karate since I was sixteen and I have added Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, Luta Livre and MMA to my repertoire since. I also enjoy the daily walks, dogtrekking, cani-cross or bikejöring sessions with the family Husky, Bellona. Add snowboarding in the winter or beach volley and mountain biking in the summer and you get a fairly good idea of my hobbies.
